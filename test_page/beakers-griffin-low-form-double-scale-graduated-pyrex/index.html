BEAKERS, GRIFFIN, LOW FORM, DOUBLE SCALE, GRADUATED, PYREX®

0Share

Beaker, with spout, manufactured with uniform wall thickness, offers optimum balance between thermal shock resistance and mechanical strength. For convenience, the 250 through 4000mL beakers have a double graduated metric scale to indicate approximate content. All sizes have an extra large marking spot. The 10mL size is not graduated.
